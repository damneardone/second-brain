A full-featured Second Brain + built-in Google Workspace module (Gog). Everything runs **completely locally** on your device—no cloud, no subscriptions, with deep self-evolution.

### 🔥 Why thousands of people are already choosing Second Brain

- **Total Privacy** — nothing ever leaves for the cloud.
- **Deep Zettelkasten** with automatic, evolving MOCs (Maps of Content).
- **Gog Workspace** — smart analysis of Gmail, Calendar, Drive, and Docs directly from screenshots.
- **Dual Self-Improvement Systems** — GEP-lite + Personal Self-Improving Layer.
- **Proactive Evolution** — the skill analyzes your history and suggests improvements on its own.
- **User Correction Loop** — learns instantly from your edits.
- **Long-term Memory** — remembers your preferences for 30–60 days.
- **Interactive Evolution Map** — a beautiful dashboard tracking skill evolution.

### ⚡ Key Advantages

| Feature                        | Second Brain                 | Cloud Alternatives          |
|-------------------------------|------------------------------|-----------------------------|
| Privacy                       | Total (on-device)            | Data in the cloud           |

### 💻 Requirements
- Device with **12 GB RAM or more** (recommended)
- **Google AI Edge Gallery** app
- **Gemma-4-E4B-it** model (3.6 GB)

---

### 🚀 Quick Install (3 minutes)

1. Open **Google AI Edge Gallery** on your device.
2. Go to **Agent Skills** → **Add Custom Skill** → **From URL**.
3. Paste the link: `https://github.com/uussnn/second-brain`
4. Select the **Gemma-4-E4B-it** model.
5. Type in the chat: `activate second brain`

**Done!** The skill will immediately start working and learning to fit your needs.
*(Alternatively: download the repository [as a ZIP archive](https://github.com/uussnn/second-brain/archive/refs/heads/main.zip) and import the local `second-brain` folder)*

### ⚡ Quick Commands

**📊 Reviews and Planning**
- `"day review"` — full breakdown of today
- `"week review"` — results and future plans
- `"what do I have today"` — quick overview of tasks and meetings

**🧠 Zettelkasten**
- `"MOC review"` — check and improve Maps of Content
- `"show MOC [topic]"` — open a specific MOC
- `"update MOCs"` — automatically update all MOCs

**🗂 Search and Memory**
- `"what did I say about X"` — find all notes on a topic
- `"show my preferences"` — look into the Personal Layer

**🧬 Evolution**
- `"show dashboard"` — open the Evolution Map
- `"personal review"` — run a personal check-up
- `"compress memory"` — cache optimization

**📧 Gog Workspace**
- `"sort mail"` — analysis of important emails
- `"what's on my calendar"` — meetings and events
- `"save to Second Brain"` — transfer data
